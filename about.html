About

I build systems that keep operations moving.

My strength is infrastructure-level development for businesses that rely on software every day. That usually means payment flows, automations, queues, internal tooling, refactoring older systems, and the kind of operational work that protects revenue and reduces manual effort.

What I optimize for Reliability, maintainability, and measurable business impact.
Where I’m strongest PHP and MySQL systems, integrations, automation, performance work, and cleanup of legacy-heavy codebases.
How I work Refactor continuously, document clearly, and solve the operational problem rather than only the visible feature request.
